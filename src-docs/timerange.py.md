## <kbd>class</kbd> `Range`
Time range to allow Jenkins to update version. 



**Attributes:**
 
 - <b>`start`</b>:  Hour to allow updates from in UTC time, in 24 hour format. 
 - <b>`end`</b>:  Hour to allow updates until in UTC time, in 24 hour format.
